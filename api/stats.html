Stats¶

Statistics and diagnostics are delegated to the ArviZ. library, a general purpose library for “exploratory analysis of Bayesian models.” For statistics and diagnostics, pymc3.<function> are now aliases for ArviZ functions. Thus, the links below will redirect you to ArviZ docs:

  • pymc3.bfmi

  • pymc3.compare

  • pymc3.ess

  • pymc3.geweke

  • pymc3.hpd

  • pymc3.loo

  • pymc3.mcse

  • pymc3.r2_score

  • pymc3.rhat

  • pymc3.summary

  • pymc3.waic
